Acknowledgements

The simulation data came from Daniel Whalen at LANL and Michael L. Norman at SDSC. The images of the data set shown on the main page came from [1].

Data storage and archiving provided by Data Central at the San Diego Supercomputer Center. Steve Cutchin and Ken Nunes were the two patient and professional people I interacted with directly. We would also like to specifically thank Mahidhar Tatineni, Krishna Muriki, and Douglas Weimer for their excellent support for simulation runs and storage.

[1] Daniel Whalen and Michael L. Norman (2008), "Ionization front instabilities in primordial H II regions," The Astrophysical Journal, 673:664–675, 2008 February 1, DOI:10.1086/524400

[2] Daniel Whalen, Brian W. O'Shea, Joseph Smidt and Michael L. Norman (2008), "Photoionization of Clustered Halos by the First Stars," In AIP Conf. Proc. 990, 381 (2008), DOI:10.1063/1.2905586

Citing Contest Data

If you use the data set from this contest in a publication or other work, please cite the data set as:

D. Whalen and M. L. Norman, "Competition data set and description," in 2008 IEEE Visualization Design Contest, 2008, http://vis.computer.org/VisWeek2008/vis/contests.html.
