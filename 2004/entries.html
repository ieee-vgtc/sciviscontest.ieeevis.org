IEEE Visualization 2004 Contest

Entries

Over 60 groups registered for access to the data. Of those, six entries were submitted. This page archives those six entries. Entrants were required to provide a video, an 2-page PDF summary of their entry, and a web page describing there contribution.

  • Jurgen P. Schulze and Andrew S. Forsberg, Brown University, USA
  • Ming Jiang, Naeem Shareef, Caixia Zhang, Roger Crawfis, Raghu Machiraju, and Han-Wei Shen, The Ohio State University, USA
  • Kenny Gruchalla and Jonathan Marbach, BP Center for Visualization, Universrity of Colorado, Bolder, USA
  • Tobias Schafhitzel, Daniel Weiskopf, and Thomas Ertl, University of Stuttgart, Germany
  • Greg P. Johnson and Chris Burns, Texas Advanced Computing Center, University of Texas, Austin, USA
  • Helmut Doleisch, Philipp Muigg, and Helwig Hauser, VRVis, Austria
