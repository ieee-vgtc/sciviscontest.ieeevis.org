Contributions

There were 6 high-quality submissions to this contest. For each paper, at least 5 reviews have been made by visualization experts and domain experts. For the best papers, two additional reviews have been done by further domain experts (see committee for details).

The winning submission and the honorable-mention submission will be announced at IEEE VisWeek 2011:
October, 25th, 2011, 11:40am, Ballroom A, Rhode Island Convention Center, Providence, USA


pdf, video		 Winner Visualizing Unsteady Vortical Behavior of a Centrifugal Pump
Mathias Otto, Alexander Kuhn, Wito Engelke, Holger Theisel
 

pdf, video		 Honorable mention Visualization of Vortex Core Differences between Ensemble Simulations
Alexis Yee Lyn Chan, Joohwi Lee, Russell M. Taylor II
